Section 01Information and company

This policy covers personal data collected through the WTM International websites, landing pages, events and social media channels. WTM International operates through two legal entities, identified by their respective Brazilian Tax IDs (CNPJs).

For any privacy-related request, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@wtmdobrasil.com.

Section 02Your rights as a data subject

Under applicable data protection law (LGPD and equivalent regulations), you may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Right to restrict the processing of your personal data
  • Right to data portability and deletion
  • Right to access and rectify inaccurate information
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to information on how your data is processed

To exercise any of these rights, write to dpo@wtmdobrasil.com. We respond within the legal timeframe.

Section 03Data we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact information — name, email, phone number
  • Professional data — job title, company name and details
  • Access & device information — IP address, cookies, browser, browsing history on our sites
  • Email interaction metrics — opens, clicks, engagement signals

We classify users in three ways: visitors (anonymous browsing), leads (forms submitted) and authenticated users with access to our platforms.

Section 04Why we process data

We process personal data primarily under the legitimate interest legal basis, for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries from prospective customers
  • Email marketing and communication of new services
  • Service promotion to qualified audiences
  • Analytics on site usage, content engagement and campaign performance

Section 05Cookies & tracking

We use analytical and advertising cookies from third-party providers, including Google Analytics, RD Station and DoubleClick. Cookie lifetimes range from one day to nine years, depending on their purpose.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain pages.

Section 06Data sharing

We share data with service providers, partners and advertisers strictly under data protection requirements, and only to the extent necessary to deliver the services you requested.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Section 07Retention & security

Personal data is retained for as long as legal requirements demand — for example, access logs may be retained for up to six months. After this period, data is anonymized or securely deleted.

We apply technical and organizational safeguards proportional to the sensitivity of the data, including encryption in transit, access controls and regular security reviews.

Section 08Privacy channel

For privacy complaints, requests under LGPD, or any concern about how we handle your data, reach out to our DPO at dpo@wtmdobrasil.com. We will acknowledge your request within five business days.
